Post Secondary Plan

After graduating high school, I plan to attend CSU Fresno State striving to earn a bachelor degree in biology. After attending a 4-year university, I hope to be accepted into an optometry school to pursue a doctorate degree in optometry. In the future, I intend on opening my own eye care practice in the Central Valley. Since there is a large proportion of people with visual impairment outnumbering the amount of optometrists in the Central Valley, I hope to help relieve the ratio, encouraging prospering optometrists all the while providing affordable care to my patients.
