THE VELDT • THE RED BALLOON

The Veldt Poster

In Web Applications, I read a short story called The Veldt, written by Ray Bradbury, a cautionary story about the threatening effects of technology. Set in the future, the story follows a typical family of four as the readers explore their daily life and discover the way technology changed their family dynamic. To convey that I was capable of analyzing text and communicating it clearly in a simplistic way, I created a poster on the effects of technology on the family using quotes from the story. Using expository writing techniques, I found two quotes that I felt were the more effective examples to showcase, along with images that assisted in expressing the message.

The Red Balloon

The Red Balloon Newspaper is an assignment designed to showcase my skills in journalistic writing. In the assignment, I created a fictional newspaper outlet whose purpose is to outline and appreciate different aspects of film that might have been overlooked by viewers. In this particular section of the newspaper, I summarized the short film, The Red Balloon, directed and written by Albert Lamorisse, and described an interpretation of the theme Lamorisse could have intended to express. In this assignment, I practiced writing in third person with the demographics of my audience in mind.
